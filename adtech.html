What is Ad Fraud?

Ad Fraud simply refers to computer-generated ad interactions created to trick publishers and advertisers into believing that the engagement with an ad is from a genuine user.

Ad fraud can be done by manipulating an engagement of the analytics data.

VeraViews is a unique solution that invalidates ad fraud in real-time while being transparent to all parties – all in a privacy-compliant way that protects all personal information.

In the video content economy, ad fraud can take the form of;

Automated repeated viewing of videos (looping views)

Loading multiple videos in parallel

Loading videos in hidden windows/tabs

Manipulating analytics

Ad fraud hurts both advertisers and publishers

Ad fraud skews the performance metrics of advertising campaigns

Ad fraud causes advertisers to lose money

Ad fraud cause publishers to lose credibility

Ad fraud stunts the growth of the advertising industry

VeraViews uses a patented Proof-of-View technology to record valid ad engagement and it stores the data immutably on a public ledger.

With VeraViews, advertisers only pay for valid engagement on ad campaigns and publishers get paid faster because the attribution data is real-time and immutable.

How VeraViews Works

Initial phase

Cross-check if the user is known before engaging.

Scoring phase

Analyzes 1st and 3rd party data gathered from phases 1 and 2 with proprietary AI. Based on the Scoring, the ad view is determined to be valid or fake.

Viewing phase

Analyse the user's behavior and device parameters without impacting the transaction or engagement.

Posting phase

Valid transactions are minted and commercial transactions are processed.
