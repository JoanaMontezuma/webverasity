What is NFT Fraud?

NFT fraud refers to the misrepresentation of a digital assets owner or the manipulation of an assets sale and transfer. NFT fraud falls into 6 main categories:

Forging of original content without the owner’s consent

Selling someone else’s legitimate NFT

Attributing NFTs to legitimate brands without consent

Duplicating NFT listings on multiple sales platforms

Failure to sell due to insufficient buyer funds

Removing access to the content after sale of an NFT

Blockchain transactions are difficult to reverse; NFT art or funds lost to frauds might be difficult to recover.
